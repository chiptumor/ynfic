# Steamed Hams

_Ding dong!_ The doorbell calls for you. Moving swiftly to the door, you pull at
your tie and brush off any speck of dust that might plague your prepared attire.

You open the door.

"Well, {{name}}, I made it... Despite your directions."

- - -

"Ah, well would you look who it is... Superintendent Chalmers! Welcome!"
You tug at your tie with a nervous grin. "I hope you're prepared for an
unforgettable luncheon!"

Chalmers waits on you to move out of the doorway before awkwardly wading past
you. "Yeah," he utters, moving to sit down at the dining table. You follow
loosely behind, parting to enter the kitchen as he tucks a napkin into his
shirt.

You can't help but hum a tune as you make way to the kitchen:
